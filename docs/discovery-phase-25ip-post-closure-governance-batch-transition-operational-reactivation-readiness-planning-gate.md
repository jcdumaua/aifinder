### Batch D — Public Launch Readiness

Batch D does not grant automatic launch authority.

## Batch transition rules

1. Each batch must have one planning or preflight gate, one execution or review gate where applicable, and one closure result.
2. Repetitive copy-forward phases are prohibited unless new evidence or a changed state exists.
3. A batch may contain multiple related checks when their dependencies require coordinated evaluation.
4. Any failed check stops the batch and preserves the last approved state.
5. No batch may silently expand into database mutation, publishing, cleanup, deployment, schema, migration, generated-type, package, lockfile, or candidate-decision work.
6. Commit and push remain separate from phase-work review until Gemini approval.
7. Human approval remains mandatory for operational reactivation and public launch.
