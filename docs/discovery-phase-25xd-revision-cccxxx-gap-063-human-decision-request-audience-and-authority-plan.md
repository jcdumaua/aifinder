# AiFinder Phase 25XD — Revision CCCXXX — GAP-063 Human Decision Request Audience and Authority Plan

## Purpose

Define who may receive and answer the future non-secret human-decision request for `GAP-063 / SEC-LR-011`.

## Authorized audience category

The request may be directed only to a human decision-maker who has authority to provide or approve:

1. The non-sensitive platform product and exact control-status surface description.
2. The non-secret read-only privilege-proof method.
3. The primary operator role designation.
4. The independent reviewer role designation.
5. The single-use authorization window.

## Authority verification requirements

Before issuance, a future gate must verify that the recipient:

- Has governance authority over the AiFinder operational decision.
- Can answer without disclosing credentials or sensitive platform identifiers.
- Understands that general approval does not authorize execution.
- Understands that all five fields require explicit answers.
- Understands that a later reviewed execution decision remains mandatory.

## Excluded recipients

Do not direct the request to:

- An automated system.
- A platform support bot.
- An unknown or unverified account.
- A person lacking AiFinder governance authority.
- A role that cannot assign both operator and reviewer responsibilities.
- A recipient who would need to reveal credentials or secret values.
